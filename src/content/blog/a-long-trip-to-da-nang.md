date: 2026-05-11T08:07:38.570Z

Today, we took my grandmother to Da Nang Airport for her trip to the US

On the way, we stopped at a Quang noodles store near Uncle No's house for breakfast

My uncle joined us for breakfast

Then, we headed to Da Nang

However, there was a problem with grandmother's name

Her ticket said "Nguyen Thi" instead of her full name, "Nguyen Thi Hong"

Because of this, she wasn't allowed to fly

We went to a restaurant for lunch and rested at the beach while Uncle Map and Aunt Em tried to find the solution

Eventually, they didn't find the common ground, so we returned to Hue
