Today, I worked the night shift with Anh

I bought a box of pork for 16k and buy-2-get-1-free seaweed for 17k with vouchers from Techcombank

While I was preparing to end the shift, a man came in and asked us where the razors were

I showed him the section and went back to counting money

But then Anh told me the man took a razor and left without paying

I urged her to chase after him

She rushed to the door and called out to him loudly

The man gave the razor back and left

It was a scary experience
