---
title: Fresh air on the riverside
date: 2026-04-06T17:13:16.038Z
---

Tonight, I walked Chien and Ben to the riverside for some fresh air

We walked along the ironwood bridge

On the way, I met Uncle Ha and his youngest son

After our walk, I gave Ben some meat and cookies

Chien also wanted them, so they started fighting

Luckily, it wasn't a serious fight

When I returned Ben to his house, the door was locked

I thought Aunt Thuy and Uncle No were out, then took Ben to my house

But Aunt Thuy called saying she was actually home

So, I drove him back to his house again
