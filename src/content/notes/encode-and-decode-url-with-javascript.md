## Encode URL

- `encodeURI()`: encode a full URL. It doesn't encode `~!@#$&*()=:/,;?+'`
- `encodeURIComponent()`: encode a part of the URL. It doesn't encode `-_.!~*'()`

## Decode URL

Use `decodeURI()` and `decodeURIComponent()` to decode corresponding encoding function's result
